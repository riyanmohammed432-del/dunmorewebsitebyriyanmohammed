A-TSI Plan

A-TSI (Targeted Support and Improvement Plan) is a designation under the Every Student Succeeds Act (ESSA) that identifies schools in need of targeted support based on the performance outcomes of specific student groups.

ARP ESSER Plan 2.5%

The 2.5% allocation for ARP ESSER funds is part of the American Rescue Plan (ARP) aimed at providing emergency relief to elementary and secondary schools affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. These funds are intended to support various programs, including evidence-based interventions to address learning loss, summer enrichment, and comprehensive after-school programs.
